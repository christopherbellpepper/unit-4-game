Crystal Collector

Instructions:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  4. Everytime a new game starts, the value of each Crystal is changed.
  5. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number set at the begining of each game.
  6. You lose if you fall short or go over.
